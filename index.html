Endless Guess

How to Play

Welcome to EndlessGuess, where you guess endlessly until you lose...

The game rules are simple: you have to select how many numbers you want to choose between before beginning the game. Then, you will be guessing a number. If your guessed number is correct, you get a point and move on! If not, you will lose 1 point and the random number will change. If you guess wrong 3 times, you will lose the game.

There is singleplayer and multiplayer mode. In singleplayer mode, you will play by yourself. In multiplayer mode, you have to play with a friend on the same device. You have to select who goes first, and the person with the most amount of points wins!

Good Luck and have fun!
